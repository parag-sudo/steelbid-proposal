01 · Executive Summary

The Opportunity

India's mid-tier steel market lacks a reliable, independent auction platform. Large players (JSW, Tata, SAIL) run proprietary platforms exclusively for their own inventory. Smaller manufacturers — Shyam Steel, Captain Steel, SRMB, and hundreds like them — have no comparable channel.

SteelBid is a cloud-based, real-time B2B auction marketplace that connects smaller steel manufacturers with verified large distributors and dealers. It replicates the experience of enterprise platforms like Mjunction and SteelEasy — at a fraction of the commission cost — while being designed from day one to handle lakh-scale concurrent bidding.

02 · Problem Statement

What Is Broken Today

Mid-tier steel companies face structural disadvantages that limit their market reach and pricing power.

🏭

No Independent Platform

Mjunction, Steelmart, and SteelEasy are either proprietary to large steel groups or focused on large enterprises, leaving hundreds of smaller manufacturers without a digital auction channel.

💸

High Commission Rates

Existing independent platforms charge 0.35%–0.75% commission. At scale, this significantly erodes seller margins, especially for commodity-grade steel where margins are already thin.

📉

Limited Buyer Reach

Without their own platforms or large sales teams, smaller manufacturers cannot reach the large network of distributors and dealers needed to maximize auction price discovery.

⚡

No Real-Time Bidding

Traditional offline auctions or email-based RFQs are slow, opaque, and non-competitive. There is no real-time bidding infrastructure accessible to mid-market sellers.

03 · Our Solution

SteelBid Platform

SteelBid is a purpose-built, real-time auction platform for the Indian steel industry. Sellers list their inventory with full specifications and photos. Verified buyers bid live with full transparency. The highest bid wins — automatically. Payments flow through the platform, and both parties are protected at every step.

04 · Platform Features

What We Are Building

Six core modules that together deliver a complete auction experience.

📣

Pre-Auction Notifications

Automated bulk outreach to all registered buyers before each auction goes live.

  • WhatsApp bulk messaging
  • Email campaigns
  • Auction preview with product specs
  • Scheduled timing reminders
📦

Product Listing Module

Rich, steel-specific listing forms with all critical dimensions and quality attributes.

  • Length, width, thickness fields
  • Shape: sheet / coil / round / tube
  • Quality grade: prime / defective / scrap
  • Photo uploads (3–4 images per lot)
  • Total quantity & base price
⚡

Live Bidding Engine

Real-time, WebSocket-powered bidding room visible to all participants simultaneously.

  • Bids in ₹100 multiples
  • Instant display to all viewers
  • 3-minute bid-alive extension
  • Simultaneous multi-lot support
  • Anti-snipe auto-extension
🔐

KYC & Access Control

Rigorous onboarding to ensure only serious, verified buyers participate in auctions.

  • GST number verification
  • Bank account validation
  • One login per company (default)
  • Paid additional logins
  • Refundable security deposit
💳

Payment & Escrow

Three-stage payment flow protecting buyers, sellers, and the platform at every transaction.

  • Registration deposit collection
  • Winner payment within 2 working days
  • Platform holds funds in escrow
  • Release to seller on dispatch
  • Deposit forfeiture on default
🔔

Post-Auction Workflow

Automated post-auction communications and delivery tracking to close the loop.

  • Winner congratulation message
  • Payment instructions & deadline
  • Seller dispatch notification
  • Buyer delivery update
  • Auction history & reporting

06 · Technical Architecture

Built for Scale & Reliability

A modern, cloud-native stack designed to handle lakh-scale concurrent users with real-time performance and 99.9% uptime.

Frontend

React + TypeScript

Fast, responsive UI with real-time WebSocket updates. Works on desktop and mobile browsers.

Real-Time Engine

WebSockets / Redis Pub-Sub

Sub-100ms bid propagation to all connected users simultaneously, regardless of volume.

Backend API

Node.js / Python FastAPI

High-throughput REST + WebSocket server with auction logic, KYC, and payment orchestration.

Database

PostgreSQL + Redis

Relational store for users, listings, bids. Redis for real-time bid cache and session management.

Messaging

WhatsApp Business API + SendGrid

Programmatic bulk WhatsApp and transactional email delivery at scale.

Payments

Razorpay / Cashfree

PCI-compliant payment gateway with escrow support, UPI, NEFT, and IMPS.

Infrastructure

AWS / GCP Cloud

Auto-scaling compute, CDN for media delivery, load balancers for high-concurrency auctions.

Storage

S3 / Cloud Storage

Fast, CDN-backed image delivery for product photos with automatic compression and resizing.

Scalability Note: The platform will be architected with horizontal scaling from day one. Even during peak auctions when lakhs of users connect simultaneously, the WebSocket infrastructure and Redis pub-sub layer ensure every bid is broadcast to all participants within milliseconds — matching the responsiveness of Mjunction-class enterprise platforms.

10 · Design Principles

What We Will Never Compromise On

Three non-negotiable pillars that define every decision on this platform.

⚡

Performance

Every bid must propagate to every viewer in under 200ms. If the platform freezes during a live auction, trust is destroyed instantly. Speed is not a feature — it is the product.

🛡️

Trust

Every buyer must be KYC-verified. Every payment must be protected. Every delivery must be tracked. A single default or fraud incident will cost us the market. Trust is earned in months, lost in minutes.

🎯

Simplicity

Steel traders are not tech users. The platform must be so simple that a first-time visitor can find the right auction, log in, and bid within 60 seconds. No tutorials. No confusion.
