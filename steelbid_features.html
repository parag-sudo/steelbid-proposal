How a Typical Auction Works

A simple, secure, and transparent transactional flow for buyers, sellers, and administrators.

1

Seller Lists Steel

Seller uploads grade, specs, tons, and reserve price.

2

Admin Approves

Operations checks certificates and publishes the auction.

3

Smart Notifications

Verified buyers are alerted on WhatsApp & Email.

4

Real-Time Auction

Buyers place bids live. Extensions prevent sniping.

5

Winner Declared

Highest bid above reserve is instantly notified.

6

Secure Escrow

Razorpay holds funds until successful delivery confirmation.
